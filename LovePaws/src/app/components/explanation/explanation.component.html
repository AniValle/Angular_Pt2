ANIMAL SHELTER



The idea of the website is to manage an animal shelter. With pages open to all visitors, only for registered users and specific for administrator users.

What motivated us to make this website is the love for animals that we both have. Imagining that we have a shelter.
We created this website to be accessible to more people and that the animals have a better chance to be adopted .

We both make decisions about the look and feel of the page and write code for the app to develop both the database aspect and the client view.
Having thus the artistic part divided between the two equally , and the technical part together for the best compression of the expected results.
